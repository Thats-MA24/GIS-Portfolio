# 🏙️ Public Facilities Hotspot Analysis (Dodoma Urban)

## 🎯 Objective
To identify spatial concentration of public facilities in Dodoma Urban using Kernel Density Estimation (KDE) for urban planning and service accessibility assessment.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}  
- Kernel Density Estimation (KDE)  
- Spatial analysis tools  

---

## 📊 Methodology
- Collection and preparation of public facility point data  
- Application of Kernel Density Estimation (KDE) to detect spatial clustering  
- Raster surface generation to visualize intensity of facility distribution  
- Map styling and interpretation in QGIS  

---

## 🗺️ Output
The resulting hotspot map shows areas with high and low concentration of public facilities in Dodoma Urban.

📄 **Map Output:** Public_Facilities_Hotspot_Map_of_Dodoma_Urban.pdf  

---

## 📌 Key Insight
The analysis highlights service concentration zones and identifies underserved areas, supporting urban planning and equitable resource distribution.
